Venue

The tenth Smart Card Research and Advanced Application IFIP Conference, will be held in Katholieke Universiteit Leuven, Leuven, Belgium. It will be co-located with ESORICS 2011 workshop.

Talks

K.U.Leuven Nieuwe Valk is situated in the heart of Leuven. Talks will be given in the auditorium 01.13. Wireless internet connection will be available to participants.

Address (see map)
K.U.Leuven Nieuwe Valk,
Auditorium 01.13
Tiensestraat 41
3000 Leuven

Lunches

Lunches for conference participants will be served at Salons Georges (see map), three minutes walking from the venue.

Conference Dinner

The CARDIS 2011 conference dinner will take place on Thursday, September 15th 2011 at the Faculty Club (see map), located in the historical Grand Beguinage (Groot Begijnhof), about 15 minutes walking from the venue.
