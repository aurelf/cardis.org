Registration Details

The registration fee is 420 €. Students register at a reduced fee of 320 €. This fee includes lunches, coffee breaks and a conference dinner. Hotel rooms need to be booked seperately.

If you register before Monday, the 15th of August 2011 you benefit from an early-bird registration discount! The early-bird registration fee is 320 €, students register at 220 €.

Proceedings are not included in the registration fee. You have the option to order the proceedings for an additional 30 € on the registration form.

Payments can be made by credit card through Ogone (only VISA and MasterCard are accepted).

Register online via the registration form.
