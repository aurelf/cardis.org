Instructions for Paper Submission

Submitted papers should represent novel contributions related to the listed topics.

They must be original, unpublished, and not submitted to another conference or journal for consideration of publication. Papers must be written in English; they should not exceed 15 pages in total. When appropriate, authors should arrange for a release for publication from their employer prior to submission. Papers accompanied by non-disclosure agreement forms will not be accepted.

Accepted papers will be presented at the conference and published in the proceedings, which will appear in Springer's Lecture Notes in Computer Science and will be available at the conference. At least one author of each accepted paper is required to register with the conference and present the paper.

Abstracts and papers must be submitted in electronic format using the electronic paper submissions tools. Only accepted formats are PDF and PostScript files. If you have any problem submitting your paper, or you may miss the deadline, please contact the administrator, by email to cardis2010@xlim.fr

Please click this link to the electronic paper submission: Electronic Paper Submission (easychair)

Submitted documents should comply to the templates or style files indicated by the publisher (also listed beneath). Please preview the manuscript in a viewer to ensure its integrity before submitting. Make sure you visually check uncommon fonts, symbols, equations, etc.
