Call for Participation

In celebration of this years DIMVA conference anniversary we would like to announce the following event highlights:

  • High-class keynote by Giovanni Vigna (UCSB and Lastline)
  • Invited talks by "FX" (Recurity) and Robert Krawczyk (BSI)
  • High quality program with 12 scientific talks
  • Welcome drinks on the evening of July 17
  • Social Event: Guided tour to the Reichstag building including a gala dinner on the top floor (Your passport will be required!)

DIMVA will take place in the Moevenpick Hotel Berlin, that is located in the centre of Berlin at Potsdamer Platz. For further information, see Venue and Accommodation.

The registration for the DIMVA conference is open. You can find detailed information at our registration webpage.

Do not miss the early bird registration deadline (June 12)!

Please note, if you need a proof of attendance, e.g., to earn Continuing Professional Education (CPE) points, the DIMVA organizing committee will sign an appropriate certificate of attendance for you.

We are looking forward to welcome you to DIMVA 2013!
