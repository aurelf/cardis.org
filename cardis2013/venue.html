Berlin

The 12th Smart Card Research and Advanced Application Conference will take place in the heart of Germany's capital. Berlin is one of the most popular urban areas in the European Union, and is well known for politics, culture and media as well as for its universities and science, respectively. It has lots of sightseeing options and attractions. As a global city, Berlin is conveniently situated. Have a look at our travel information to plan your trip to Berlin.

Our Conference Hotel





The surprisingly different 4 star plus Moevenpick Hotel Berlin is located in the centre of Berlin at Potsdamer Platz. For its design concept it was honoured with the Berlin Real Estate Award 2005.
The exceptionally innovative interior design and a kaleidoscope of colours, shapes and materials connect with the historical flair of the Siemens Building. There are 243 rooms, a Tower Suite and 18 studio rooms that feature angled ceilings and a free-standing bathtub.
