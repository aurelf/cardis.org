Conference Registration

Registration is now open for CARDIS 2008.

Delegates wishing to attend should send us a completed registration form along with the relevant documentation required for their chosen method of payment as per the instructions contained in the registration form. Delegates can pay by credit card and bank transfer up to the 25th of August. After this date the only accepted method of payment will be cash at the conference venue.
