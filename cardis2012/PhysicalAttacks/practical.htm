How to get there

You can find a map with markings of important places here. A brochure of sights of Graz is available from Graz Tourismus.

By plane

The airport of Graz is connected via the hubs Vienna, Munich and Frankfurt by Lufthansa and partner airlines. Budget travelers might consider Ryanair or AirBerlin, which offer connections from London Stansted or Berlin respectively. Taxis from the airport to the center of Graz are about 15-20€. Alternatively, buses connect the airport with the main station (Graz Hauptbahnhof) and the Jakomini Platz.

Another option for budget travelers is to fly to Vienna and to take the train to Graz afterwards (plan 3h for that). Trains between Graz and Vienna operate on an hourly basis. Timetables can be found here.

By train

Leave the train at Graz main station (Graz Hauptbahnhof). Use any of the following tram lines: #6 (towards St. Peter), line #7 (towards St. Leonhard/LKH), line #3 (towards Krenngasse) or line #1 (towards Mariatrost) and exit at Südtirolerplatz/Kunsthaus, an overview of all tram lines is available here. From there it is a very short walk to the conference hotel and conference venue. Ticket prices: 1h for 1,90€, 24h for 4,20€. Alternatively, Taxis offer their services at the main station as well.

By car

Highways A2 and A9 have several exits for Graz. For using the highways, you need a toll sticker (Vignette), which you can buy at every gas station. In Graz, the Weitzer Citygarage can be used by all participants of the conferrence for 10,50€ per day, please pay the fee a the reception of the Weitzer Hotel.
