CARDIS 2014: Registration

Registration form

Registration is done by filling in this form and sending it to celine.carle@cnam-iledefrance.fr and pierre.paradinas@cnam.fr with the required information.

Conference fee

The registration fee for CARDIS 2014 is as follows. This includes admission to all technical sessions, proceedings, lunches, social event, conference dinner, and coffee breaks.


 Early bird (by October 5) After October 5
Student registration 200€ 300€
Regular registration 300€ 400€

Payment

Only payment by

  • Credit card (VISA or MASTERCARD) or
  • Fund tranfer to organization

is accepted.

An invoice will be sent by email or provided to you during the conference.

Cancellation policy

No reimbursement will be possible.

Hotel reservation

No rooms were pre-booked for CARDIS 2014. There are plenty of hotels in Paris, depending on your budget. We recommend you to use your favorite application or website to find a hotel that best suits your needs.

The conference venue is: 2 Rue Conté, 75003 Paris
