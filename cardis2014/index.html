13th Smart Card Research and Advanced Application Conference

Paris, France, November 5-7, 2014

CARDIS, first held in Lille, France in 1994, this year turns 20. Over these years, as smart cards have become a pervasive, foundational technology for bootstrapping trust in our society, CARDIS has become the foremost international academic conference dedicated to research on smart cards and their applications.
