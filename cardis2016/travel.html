Airport

Nice Côte d'Azur International Airport is a little over 25km from Cannes. IATA code is NCE. Direct connections to more than 100 cites across Europe, North America, North Africa and the Middle East, served by over 50 airlines. France's second busiest airport.

http://en.nice.aeroport.fr/

Buses

Acces from the aiport to Cannes by bus is easy. There are two possibilities.

210 - EXPRESS NICE AÉROPORT / CANNES

Easiest and fastest option operated by Lignes d'Azur CG06 leaving from platform 3 in Terminal 1 and platform 3 in Terminal 2. Tickets cost 22€ and the service operates from 8.00am to 10.00pm.

http://en.nice.aeroport.fr/Passengers/DIRECTIONS-PARKING/Gestion-Bus-et-Navettes/Bus-lines/Bus-Nice-Cannes-210-Express

https://www.departement06.fr/documents/A-votre-service/Deplacements/transports-en-commun/210-mai2016.pdf

The venue is just in front of Cannes - Rue des Serbes and a 10-minute walk from Cannes - Hôtel de Ville.

200 - CANNES

Slower, more difficult option operated by Lignes d'Azur CG06 leaving from the Aéroport / Promenade des Anglais, accessible by foot (5 mintues) from Terminal 1 - no departure from the airport itself. Tickets from 1.50€. Services stop in Cannes - Gare SNCF and runs from 6.05am until 9.45pm.

https://www.departement06.fr/documents/A-votre-service/Deplacements/transports-en-commun/dpt06-cadredevie_lignes_200.pdf

The venue is an 3-minute walk from Cannes - Gare SNCF.

On Thursday, Friday and Saturday, line 200 also operates during night starting from the airport, platform 3 in Terminal 1.

http://en.nice.aeroport.fr/Passengers/DIRECTIONS-PARKING/Gestion-Bus-et-Navettes/Bus-lines/Bus-Nice-Cannes-N200

https://www.departement06.fr/documents/Import/servir-les-habitants/noctambus_200.pdf

Train

From Nice Airport

There is frequent train service between Nice Saint Augustin and Cannes. Nice Saint Augustin is accessible from the airport either by foot (10 mins) or by a combination of free shuttle service and then a short walk (5 mins). Tickets are 5.90€. The service runs from approximately 5.20am until 9.15pm.

http://en.nice.aeroport.fr/Passengers/DIRECTIONS-PARKING/Getting-to-the-airport/Trains

http://en.nice.aeroport.fr/content/download/129611/3395757/file/St%20Augustin%20V2.pdf

The venue is an 3-minute walk from Cannes - Gare SNCF.

From Paris

There also is a quite frequent train service between Paris and Cannes.

http://en.voyages-sncf.com/en/

The venue is an 3-minute walk from Cannes - Gare SNCF.

Taxis

Available outside both Terminals. Expect to pay between 70 and 80 Euros each way between 07.00 and 19.00 and between 80 and 90 Euros from 19.00 to 07.00. Supplements not included.

http://en.nice.aeroport.fr/Passengers/DIRECTIONS-PARKING/Getting-to-the-airport/Taxis-nicois
