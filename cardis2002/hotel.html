Hotel, Parking, and Transportation Information

Hotel Reservation Discount Deadline: October 30, 2002

Why should you stay in the headquarter hotel?
We would like to encourage you to stay in the headquarter hotel(s) and when making your reservation remember to state that you are attending the USENIX conference.

USENIX conferences use a lot of meeting space and rental fees can be quite costly. As a result, USENIX always negotiates contracts that contain guarantees that a certain number of sleeping rooms will be used during our conferences. When those guarantees are not met, USENIX is assessed penalties.

As a benefit to you, USENIX is able to leverage these guarantees to negotiate discounted rates for your stay. Widespread defection from the headquarters hotel will inevitably lead to hotels declining to book USENIX conferences and also higher conference registration fees.

Although we try to negotiate the best possible rates, we recognize that not everyone can afford to stay in the headquarters hotel. We always try to choose sites that have less expensive options in the immediate area.

Thank you for your understanding.

Airport and Transportation

San Jose International Airport (SJC)
Located approximately 6 miles (10 kilometers) from the Hyatt Sainte Claire hotel.

Shuttles can be found in one of two areas:
Terminal A — In the garage outside of baggage claim
Terminal C — Outside, same side of street, just south of baggage claim
Fares range from $9 to $12 USD, per person, one-way.
A taxi from SJC to the Hyatt Sainte Claire will cost approximately $14 USD.
For more information about transportation to and from the San Jose airport, please go to www.sjc.org.

San Francisco International Airport (SFO)
Located approximately 35 miles (57 kilometers) from the Hyatt Sainte Claire hotel.

A taxi from SFO to the Hyatt Sainte Claire will cost approximately $120 USD.

Shuttles can be found in one of two areas:
Domestic Terminals:Vans depart from level 2 (departures), Center Island, outside all terminals.
International Terminal:Vans depart from level 3 (departures), Center Island, outside the terminal. Fares range from $18 to $40 USD, per person, one-way.

For more information about transportation to and from the San Francisco airport, please go to www.flysfo.com.

Parking

Valet parking is available at the Hyatt Sainte Claire for $15 per night, which includes in-and-out privileges. Public parking is also available in a parking garage connected to the San Jose Convention Center, just 1 block West of the hotel. The fee is $10 per night and does not include in-and-out privileges.
