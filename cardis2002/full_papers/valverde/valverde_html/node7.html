Description of the Advanced Encryption Standard

In this section we briefly describe the Advanced Encryption Standard (AES). For a more detailed description we refer to [DR2]. AES encrypts plaintexts consisting of lb bytes, where lb, or . AES uses a secret key, called cipher key, consisting of lk bytes, where lk or . Any combination of values lb and lk is allowed.
