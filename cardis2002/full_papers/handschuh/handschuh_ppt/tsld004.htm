The Personalization Problem

  • The parties are as follows :

    • a secret-less SIM card to be personalized
    • a customer (telecom operator)
    • a personalization system (PC + DC9000) the customer has no reason to trust

  • Goal :

    • 1. At end of process, card must contain secret KI
    • 2. Customer knows pair (IMSI, KI) (integrity)
    • 3. Customer is the only one to share secret KI with card (confidentiality)
