Card Process Flow

    Injection of ABS plastic material to form the body of the card.

    Printing of an image on a card.

    Graphic and electric personalization according to customer specifications, printing of mailers for cardholders & Value Added Packaging (VAP)

    The micromodule cavity is machined to specific dimensions.

    Embedding: the module is inserted and glued in the cavity.

    Test coding: an electrical test is carried out and the embedded module is encoded.

    Cutting to plug-in format for mobile phones.

    Guarantee that the finished product complies with customer specifications.
