    THE CITIZEN CARD AS:

    - An extended ID card

    - A general access card to administration

    and government services

    Need for more security and demonstration of citizenship (Citizen Card,

    Driving License, Passport…..),

    Access to more government services (Health, Social Security, Education…

    Capability to personalize the user’s profile,

    A component of the emerging concept of E-Democracy (Voting, polling,….),

    A candidate to the function of ESE (E-State Employee).

    A challenge for the politicians and the jurists… but we already

    have most of the required tools.
