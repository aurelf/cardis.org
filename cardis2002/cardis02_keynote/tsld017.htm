    It must be totally different from any existing one.

    An then not to look as an extension of communication or money management offers

    It must apply to immense populations

    And possibly with some international possibilities

    It must offer a high level of security for services providing

    It must require a high level of personalization

    But present a common standardized kernel for applications

    Especially for shared data and functions (encryption, data management and communication

    It must be sufficiently cheap for being distributed for free

    Providing that the cost will be moved to the services themselves

    Or that the service provider will save a lot of money by using a card.

    I must be socially accepted

    It must offer real services to the customer

    and not only money saving or security to the service provider

    It must be compulsory to access offered services

    But must provide facilities for stolen or lost cards.

    It would be preferable to think about a multi-application card

    but with one main issuer.

    Any technical or application failure must be guaranteed by the card issuer
